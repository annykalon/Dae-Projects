About Us

We are passionate candle makers based in New Haven, dedicated to creating candles that not only light up your space but also fill it with delightful fragrances. Our journey began with a love for crafting and a desire to bring warmth and coziness to every home. Each candle is carefully hand-poured and made with high-quality, eco-friendly materials. Whether you're looking to relax after a long day or set the mood for a special occasion, our candles are perfect for creating the ambiance you desire.
